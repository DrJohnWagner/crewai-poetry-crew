### Doorway Between Mercy And Breaking
By The CrewAI Poetry Crew

```markdown
you stand in a room that won't quit
    crowding your lungs
the pressure gnaws at your throat,
    a knot you mistake for yourself
then the knot leans, tight and stubborn, and opens a doorway
    inside you
    a doorway breathing softly, inviting you to stay and listen
you step through, listening for the sound your own heartbeat makes
    when it knows it's safe
the doorway glows
    with patient light
    not mercy
    but evidence that you exist
the knot loosens its grip, just enough for words to form,
    slow and careful
you hear a voice that sounds like your own,
    asking you to stay, to hear what you forgot
the room tilts, but you do not break--
    only bend toward breath and rain and morning
you remain, and the doorway remains, a threshold you carry
    like a pocketknife between teeth
```
